Empowering Lives Through UpSkilling.

Enable G, founded in 2021 by David Hay, is a non-profit empowering individuals through personal and professional development. Originating in Cape Town, it has expanded to Bali and South/Central America.

The organization focuses on life skills, career growth, English proficiency, and business training, with a special emphasis on young adults. Graduates are encouraged to give back by sharing their knowledge within their communities, fostering local support networks.

Enable G’s mission is to create global impact through skill-building, cultural exchange, and community empowerment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Enable G's programs and how to get involved.

What is Enable G?

Enable G is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing free personal and professional development programs worldwide.

Who can benefit from Enable G's programs?

Anyone looking to improve their personal skills, career growth, or network globally can benefit from our programs.

How much do Enable G programs cost?

All our programs are offered at no cost to participants, thanks to generous support from donors and volunteers.

How can I get involved with Enable G?

You can join as a participant, volunteer to support our mission, or donate to help us expand our reach.

Where is Enable G located?

We have a presence in South Africa, Bali, and South/Central America. Our programs are accessible worldwide online.
