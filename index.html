Empowering Lives Through UpSkilling.

Enable G, founded in 2021 by David Hay, is a non-profit empowering individuals through personal and professional development. Originating in Cape Town, it has expanded to Bali and South/Central America.

The organization focuses on life skills, career growth, English proficiency, and business training, with a special emphasis on young adults. Graduates are encouraged to give back by sharing their knowledge within their communities, fostering local support networks.

Enable G’s mission is to create global impact through skill-building, cultural exchange, and community empowerment.

FAQs

1. What is Enable G?

Enable G is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering individuals through personal and professional skill development. We offer guidance, resources, and training to improve lives and foster career growth at no cost.

2. Where is Enable G located?

Enable G is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering individuals through personal and professional skill development. We offer guidance, resources, and training to improve lives and foster career growth.

3. Who can benefit from Enable G’s programs?

Everyone! Our programs are designed to offer guidance and training to improve personal skills, career development and more.

4. What kind of support does Enable G provide?

We help individuals improve life balance, relationships, finances, English proficiency, and business skills like marketing and sales.

5. How can I get involved with Enable G?

You can join as a participant to develop your skills or as a volunteer to support our mission. Graduates are encouraged to give back by sharing their learning with their communities.

6. How does Enable G support local communities?

We foster local support networks by encouraging program graduates to share their skills and knowledge, creating a cycle of growth and empowerment within their communities.

7. How can I donate to Enable G?

You can support our mission by donating through our website. Every contribution helps us reach more individuals and expand our impact globally.

8. How can I contact Enable G?

Visit our Contact Us page or email us directly for more information about our programs, volunteering, or donations.

9. Is there a fee to participate in Enable G programs?

Most of our programs are offered at no cost to participants, thanks to donations and support from our community.

10. How do I stay updated on Enable G’s activities?

Follow us on social media or subscribe to our newsletter for updates on programs, events, and success stories.
