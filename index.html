About Us

Enable G is a non-profit organization dedicated to equipping individuals with personal and business skills to transform their lives. Founded by David Hay in 2021 after his retirement as a businessman and entrepreneur, Enable G began in Cape Town, South Africa, and has since expanded to Bali and across South and Central America.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals through skill development, providing the guidance and resources needed for personal and professional growth. We believe in building local support communities where individuals who receive support can give back by sharing their knowledge and skills with others.
